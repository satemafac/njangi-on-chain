Data Deletion Request Form

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon processing your request, we will delete:

  • Profile information and preferences
  • Join request history and notifications
  • Session data and authentication tokens
  • Communication logs and support tickets
  • Analytics data linked to your account

What Data Cannot Be Deleted?

The following data cannot be deleted due to technical or legal constraints:

  • Blockchain transaction records on Sui network
  • Smart contract interaction history
  • Data required for legal compliance and fraud prevention
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data

Data Deletion Process

  1. Request Submission: Submit this form or email us directly
  2. Identity Verification: We may ask for additional verification to confirm your identity
  3. Circle Status Check: We'll verify you have no active circles or pending transactions
  4. Processing: Data deletion will be processed within 30 days
  5. Confirmation: You'll receive confirmation once deletion is complete

Alternative Contact Methods

Email: privacy@njangi-on-chain.com

Subject Line: Data Deletion Request - [Your Email]

Response Time: Within 72 hours for acknowledgment, up to 30 days for completion

What to Include in Your Email:

  • Your account email address
  • Wallet address (if known)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
  • Confirmation that you understand blockchain data limitations

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about the data deletion process or our data handling practices, please contact us at privacy@njangi-on-chain.com or refer to our Privacy Policy.

This data deletion policy is part of our commitment to user privacy and data protection rights.
